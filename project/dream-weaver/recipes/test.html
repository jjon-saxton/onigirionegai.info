Seven Hearts Test Scene

Background information

This scene is set sometime between Yui and Azumi's stories. A mysterious black Kitsune, who identifies himself as Kuroko, appears to Yui after she begins to notice her heart seed is no longer glowing as bright as it had when she first became a magical girl and she wonders what would happen if the light in the seed went out.
